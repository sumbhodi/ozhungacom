THE KOSHAS

Panchakoshaviveka  ·  The Five Sheaths of Being
In Vedantic philosophy, five sheaths of Maya veil the Atman — the eternal, undivided Self.

1ANNAMAYA KOSHA
The Food Body
The body of flesh, bone & earth.
Nourished by food. The outermost veil.
2PRANAMAYA KOSHA
The Vital Body
Breath and life force — prana.
The energy that animates all form.
3MANOMAYA KOSHA
The Mental Body
Thoughts, feelings & sensation.
The mind's endless restless current.
4VIJNANAMAYA KOSHA
The Wisdom Body
Intellect, reason & discernment.
The witnessing awareness behind mind.
5ANANDAMAYA KOSHA
The Bliss Body
Causeless joy & dreamless sleep.
The innermost veil near pure Being.
ATMAN
The True Self — Pure Consciousness
Eternal, boundless, without attribute — not a layer, but the ground beneath all layers.
The Atman is what remains when every sheath of Maya is seen through and set aside.
Tat tvam asi  ·  Thou art That
